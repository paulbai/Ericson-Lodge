Our Story

A Legacy of Warm
Sierra Leonean Hospitality

Ericson Lodge is a comfortable, homely, and welcoming accommodation situated away from the busy main road of Kenema, in a quiet and relaxing area of the city. Despite its peaceful setting, it remains conveniently close to shopping areas and key points of interest.

Established in 2010 and owned by a Sierra Leonean family, the lodge brings a warm, personal touch to hospitality in the Eastern Province. We've built a reputation for cleanliness, warm service, value for money, and well-maintained facilities.

Explore Kenema

Kenema is the gateway to Sierra Leone's spectacular eastern interior — a region of lush rainforests, rich traditions, and enduring spirit.

Discover More
Nature

Gola Rainforest

A UNESCO Biosphere Reserve — one of West Africa's most biodiverse forests. Ideal for ecotourism and hiking.

Culture

Central Market

Diamonds, palm oil, cocoa, and everyday goods. A fascinating window into provincial commerce.

Heritage

Mende Villages

Experience traditional dance, music, weaving, palm wine tapping, and storytelling in nearby villages.
