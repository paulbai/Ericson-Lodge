01 — About Us

A Legacy of Warmth Since 2010

Ericson Lodge is a comfortable, stylish hotel nestled in the heart of Kenema — Sierra Leone's vibrant Eastern Province capital. Founded and family-operated since 2010, we bring a warm, personal touch to hospitality that has earned us recognition as one of Kenema's most trusted and recommended places to stay.

Set away from the busy main road in a quiet, relaxing area, our lodge offers a peaceful retreat while keeping you conveniently close to the city's shopping areas and key points of interest.
